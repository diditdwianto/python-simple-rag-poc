# RainbowCandy

RainbowCandy is a colorful chewy candy made by the fictional company Lumina Sweets,
first launched in 2021. Each piece is a small cube that changes flavor as you chew,
moving through a sequence of fruit tastes. RainbowCandy is sold worldwide and is
known for its bright packaging and its slogan, "Taste the whole rainbow in one bite."

## Flavors and colors

A single pack contains seven colors, each with its own flavor:

- Red — strawberry
- Orange — tangerine
- Yellow — pineapple
- Green — green apple
- Blue — blueberry
- Indigo — blackcurrant
- Violet — grape

The signature feature is the "color-shift" center: the violet (grape) piece starts
sweet and finishes slightly sour, which the company markets as its most popular
flavor.

## Packaging and sizes

- Mini Pack: 30 grams, about 12 pieces. Retail price: USD 1.20.
- Standard Pack: 90 grams, about 36 pieces. Retail price: USD 2.90.
- Party Bag: 400 grams, resealable. Retail price: USD 9.50.

## Ingredients and dietary info

RainbowCandy is made with sugar, glucose syrup, fruit juice concentrates, natural
and artificial flavors, and plant-based food coloring. It is gluten-free and
vegetarian. It is NOT vegan, because it contains gelatin. Each Standard Pack
contains about 350 calories.

Allergen note: RainbowCandy is produced in a facility that also processes nuts and
soy. People with nut allergies should check the label.

## Availability and fun facts

RainbowCandy is available in supermarkets, convenience stores, and the official
online store at shop.luminasweets.example. A limited-edition "Midnight RainbowCandy"
edition, which glows faintly under UV light, is released every October for
Halloween. The candy is free shipping on online orders over USD 15.
