# Indo-Trader-Inc Product Catalog

All coffees are sold as whole bean or ground (choose at checkout) in 250g and 1kg
bags. Prices below are the standard retail price for a 250g bag.

## Single-Origin Beans

### Sumatra Mandheling — Dark Roast
- SKU: ITC-SUM-250 / ITC-SUM-1KG
- Origin: North Sumatra, 1,200–1,500 m altitude
- Process: wet-hulled (Giling Basah)
- Tasting notes: earthy, herbal, dark chocolate, low acidity, full body
- Price: Rp 190,000 (USD 12.50) per 250g; Rp 680,000 (USD 45.00) per 1kg

### Aceh Gayo — Medium Roast (Organic)
- SKU: ITC-ACE-250 / ITC-ACE-1KG
- Origin: Gayo highlands, Aceh, 1,300–1,600 m
- Tasting notes: milk chocolate, citrus, brown sugar, balanced acidity
- Price: Rp 210,000 (USD 13.80) per 250g; Rp 760,000 (USD 50.00) per 1kg
- Note: this is the company's best-selling coffee.

### Toraja Sulawesi — Medium-Dark Roast
- SKU: ITC-TOR-250 / ITC-TOR-1KG
- Origin: Tana Toraja, Central Sulawesi, 1,400–1,800 m
- Tasting notes: warm spice, dark chocolate, ripe fruit, syrupy body
- Price: Rp 220,000 (USD 14.50) per 250g

### Java Preanger — Medium Roast
- SKU: ITC-JAV-250 / ITC-JAV-1KG
- Origin: Preanger, West Java, 1,000–1,400 m
- Tasting notes: roasted nuts, caramel, smooth, mild acidity
- Price: Rp 185,000 (USD 12.20) per 250g

### Bali Kintamani — Light-Medium Roast (Organic)
- SKU: ITC-BAL-250 / ITC-BAL-1KG
- Origin: Kintamani, Bali, 1,200–1,600 m
- Tasting notes: bright citrus, floral, lemongrass, clean finish
- Price: Rp 205,000 (USD 13.50) per 250g

### Flores Bajawa — Medium Roast
- SKU: ITC-FLO-250 / ITC-FLO-1KG
- Origin: Bajawa, Flores, 1,200–1,600 m
- Tasting notes: caramel, hazelnut, chocolate, medium body
- Price: Rp 200,000 (USD 13.20) per 250g
