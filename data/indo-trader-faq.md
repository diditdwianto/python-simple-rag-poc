# Indo-Trader-Inc Frequently Asked Questions

## Are your coffees whole bean or ground?
Every coffee is available as whole bean or pre-ground. You choose the grind at
checkout. We recommend whole bean and grinding just before brewing for the best
flavor.

## Do you ship internationally?
Yes. International orders ship via DHL Express and typically arrive in 5–10
business days. Import duties and taxes are the customer's responsibility.

## How fresh is the coffee?
All coffee is roasted to order in small batches and shipped within 48 hours of
roasting. The roast date is printed on every bag. We recommend using the beans
within 2–4 weeks of that date.

## Is your coffee certified organic?
Two lines are certified organic: Aceh Gayo and Bali Kintamani. The other
single-origin coffees are sourced directly from partner farmers but are not
organic-certified.

## Do you offer a subscription?
Yes. The monthly home subscription delivers your chosen coffee every month with a
15% discount and free domestic shipping. You can pause or cancel anytime from your
account.

## How do I become a wholesale customer?
Email wholesale@indo-trader.example with your business details and tax ID (NPWP).
Wholesale pricing is 20% off catalog for orders of 10kg or more per month.

## What is your return policy?
We offer a freshness guarantee: report damaged or stale beans within 14 days of
delivery for a free replacement. Opened bags are not refundable except for quality
defects.

## Which coffee do you recommend for espresso?
Sumatra Mandheling and Toraja Sulawesi work best for espresso — they are low in
acidity, full-bodied, and pair well with milk.

## Which coffee is best for someone who likes bright, fruity flavors?
Try Bali Kintamani (citrus and floral) or Aceh Gayo (chocolate and citrus). These
are lighter and more acidic than the earthy Sumatra and Sulawesi coffees.
