# About Indo-Trader-Inc

Indo-Trader-Inc is an Indonesian specialty coffee company founded in 2015 and
headquartered in Bandung, West Java. The company sources, roasts, and sells
single-origin Indonesian coffee to customers in Indonesia and abroad. As of 2025
it employs around 140 people and operates its own roastery in Bandung plus a
distribution warehouse in Surabaya.

The company's mission is to bring high-quality Indonesian coffee directly from
smallholder farmers to cups around the world while paying farmers fair prices.
Indo-Trader-Inc works directly with more than 600 partner farmers across seven
growing regions: Aceh (Gayo highlands), North Sumatra (Mandheling), West Java
(Preanger), Central Sulawesi (Toraja), Bali (Kintamani), Flores (Bajawa), and
East Java (Ijen).

Indo-Trader-Inc holds organic certification for its Aceh Gayo and Bali Kintamani
lines and is a member of the Specialty Coffee Association (SCA). All coffee is
roasted to order in small batches to maximize freshness, and the company ships
beans within 48 hours of roasting.

Leadership: the company was co-founded by Rina Wijaya (CEO) and Bayu Pratama
(Head of Sourcing). Customer support can be reached at support@indo-trader.example
and the wholesale team at wholesale@indo-trader.example.
