# Indo-Trader-Inc Sales, Pricing & Shipping

## Sales channels
Indo-Trader-Inc sells through four channels: its online store
(shop.indo-trader.example), wholesale to cafes and offices, export to overseas
distributors, and a monthly home-subscription service.

## Customer tiers and discounts
- Retail: standard catalog prices, no minimum order.
- Subscription: a recurring monthly delivery receives a 15% discount and free
  domestic shipping.
- Wholesale: for orders of 10kg or more per month, customers receive 20% off
  catalog prices. Wholesale onboarding requires a business tax ID (NPWP).
- Distributor/export: custom pricing negotiated per contract, minimum order
  100kg per shipment.

## Promotions
- Free domestic shipping on retail orders over Rp 500,000 (USD 33).
- First-time customers get 10% off with code WELCOME10.
- Bulk bean bundles (3x 1kg) include a 12% discount.

## Shipping
- Domestic (Indonesia): 2–4 business days via JNE and SiCepat.
- International: 5–10 business days via DHL Express; customer pays duties.
- Orders are roasted to order and dispatched within 48 hours.

## Sales performance (fiscal year 2024)
- Total revenue: Rp 45 billion (approximately USD 2.9 million).
- Year-over-year growth: 30% versus 2023.
- Best-selling product: Aceh Gayo Medium Roast, about 28% of bean volume.
- Export markets: 12 countries, led by Japan, Australia, and the Netherlands.
- Subscription base: roughly 4,200 active monthly subscribers at year end.
- Wholesale accounts: 180 active cafe and office accounts.

## Returns and freshness
Indo-Trader-Inc offers a freshness guarantee: if beans arrive damaged or past
their roast-freshness window, customers may request a replacement within 14 days
of delivery. Opened bags are not eligible for refund except for quality defects.
